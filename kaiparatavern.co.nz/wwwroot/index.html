About Us

The Kaipara Tavern is Helensville's local pub. Featuring great events, good drinking and dining facilities and a relaxed atmosphere, make sure you visit the Kaipara Tavern the next time you find yourself in Helensville. We are open for lunch, and dinner. We also serve coffee and drinks. We have a lot of parking space and are priced between $10- $30. Children are also welcome.
We are open Mon-Wed from 10.00-21.00, Thur from 10.00-00.00. On Fri-Sat we are open from 10.00-01.00 and on Sun from 11.00-21.00.
