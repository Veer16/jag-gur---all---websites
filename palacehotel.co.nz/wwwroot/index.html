Welcome to Palace Hotel

Palace Hotel offers comfortable, affordable accommodation right in the heart of Te Aroha with amazing views looking directly at the mountain. Pub, beds at $60 with bar, gaming lounge, and a family restaurant. Television in all rooms; secure bike and gear storage. Ideal for group bookings.

Here you can enjoy:

COFFEE: The fresh Urge Espresso Coffee (and other non-alcoholic options),

BEER: Waikato Draught, Lion Red, RTD's, Spirits, Cocktails,

FOOD: Home of the $12 Lunch! Great food at realistic prices,

ENTERTAINMENT: Gambling, Pokies, TAB, Sky Sports & Pool tables etc.,

MUSIC: Live music, DJs, Karaoke and Juke Box,

OUTDOOR VIEW AREA : Sit outside under cover, enjoy the lovely view while you sip your coffee or indulge in a delicious meal.
