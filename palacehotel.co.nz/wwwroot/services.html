Rooms

Palace Hotel offers comfortable, affordable accommodation right in the heart of Te Aroha with amazing views looking directly at the mountain. Pub, beds at $60 with bar, gaming lounge, and a family restaurant. Television in all rooms; secure bike and gear storage. Ideal for group bookings.

Bar & Restaurants

Fresh "Urge" coffee made to order with a full range of coffee options and cakes to enjoy. Come and relax in our outdoor courtyard when it's warm or stay cozy next to our indoor fire during those cold winter months. Restaurant open 7 days for lunch and dinner. Come and experience our world famous (in Te Aroha) scotch steak, seafood basket, pork belly, ribs, shanks and much more!! Delicious "over-the-top" desserts are a favorite for the locals.

TAb, Pokies & Gaming Zone

Come and enjoy a chilled beer with Gambling, Pokies, TAB, Sky Sports & Pool tables etc. Waikato Draught, Lion Red, RTD's, Spirits, Cocktails and more. Sit outside under cover, enjoy the lovely view while you sip your coffee/beer or indulge in a delicious meal.
